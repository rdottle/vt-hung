Vt Hung

contactvthung @ gmail.com

Vt Hung was born in Dorchester, Massachusetts. His refugee parents opened the first Vietnamese grocery store in Boston. He attended Boston Public Schools, B.C. High, Boston College, and the Lynch School of Education. After completing his M.Ed. in Secondary Education and English, he worked in Boston and New York City schools. He is a Kundiman Fiction Fellow and a former Margins Fellow at the Asian American Writers' Workshop. He is currently a fiction candidate in Syracuse University's MFA program.
